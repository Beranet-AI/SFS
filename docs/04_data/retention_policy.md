# Data Retention Policy

This policy defines how long different data types are retained.

---

## Telemetry
- High-frequency telemetry retained for configurable period
- Aggregated summaries may be retained longer
- Raw telemetry may be archived or deleted

---

## Health Records
- Retained for the lifetime of livestock
- Used for trend analysis and auditing

---

## Incidents
- Retained indefinitely
- Required for compliance and auditing

---

## Logs
- Operational logs retained per environment policy
- Security logs retained longer

---

## Compliance Notes

- Retention policies are configurable
- Deletion jobs must be auditable
- No silent data loss is permitted
